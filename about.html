About Vijayadurga Aquafoods

Delivering Fresh, Premium Seafood Directly to Your Doorstep Since Day One

Our Story

Vijayadurga Aquafoods was founded with a simple mission: to bring the freshest, highest-quality seafood directly from trusted farms to your kitchen. We believe that everyone deserves access to premium aquatic products without compromising on quality or convenience.

Starting from humble beginnings, we've grown into a trusted name in the seafood delivery industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across our service areas. Our commitment to excellence has never wavered.

Today, we continue to uphold the values that made us successful: transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Quality Standards

We maintain the highest standards of food safety and quality across our entire supply chain:

  • 100% Fresh: All products are sourced fresh every morning from certified farms and suppliers
  • Premium Selection: Each product undergoes rigorous quality checks before reaching our warehouses
  • Cold Chain Management: We maintain strict cold chain protocols from farm to your doorstep to preserve freshness
  • Hygienic Packaging: All products are packed in food-grade, insulated containers with ice packs
  • FSSAI Certified: Our operations comply with all food safety regulations and certifications
  • Expert Handling: Our team is trained in proper handling and storage of aquatic products
